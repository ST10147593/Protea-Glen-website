About Us

Protea Glen Youth Development Centre provides after-school learning and enrichment programs that help learners grow academically and personally.

We believe in safe, structured, and supportive learning environments. Our staff are trained and screened, and our programs are shaped to build skills and confidence.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To nurture a community of young learners who are confident, curious, and empowered to reach their full potential through quality tutoring, care, and support.

Our Mission

To provide accessible and affordable educational support by offering personalized tutoring, daily learning programs, and holistic growth opportunities for all learners.
