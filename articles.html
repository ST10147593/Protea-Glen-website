How to Build an Effective Study Routine

Building a study routine helps students manage their time and reduce stress. Start by creating a consistent schedule — plan specific times for study and rest. Break large topics into smaller sections and use short, focused study sessions of about 30–45 minutes followed by 10-minute breaks. Prioritize subjects that need the most attention and review daily. Finally, remove distractions like phones and create a quiet, organized study space.


How Parents Can Support Their Children at Home

Parents can create a supportive learning environment by setting a daily routine, ensuring their children have a quiet place to study, and encouraging curiosity. Discuss their school day, review homework together, and celebrate small achievements. Show genuine interest in what they learn — it builds confidence and helps them stay motivated.
